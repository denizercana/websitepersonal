Design Process

Evidence-based, collaborative, and iteration-friendly.

1
Clarify outcomes.
Backward design with measurable objectives & alignment to standards.
2
Prototype & storyboard.
Low-fi flows → high-fi assets; plan activities and assessments.
3
Accessibility pass.
Alt text, color contrast, captions, transcripts, cognitive load checks.
4
Pilot & iterate.
Collect learner/TA feedback, analyze, refine, and document.
